## Alarm++

Alarm++ is an alarm clock that actually wakes you up.
It works by not letting you dismiss the alarm before you complete a game that the app has given you.

## Features
* alarm clock
  * create/edit/delete alarms
  * select on witch days alarm rings
  * select at what time alarm rings
  * activate/disable alarms
* 4 games
  * jumping jacks
  * go into light
  * balance balls
  * point at color
* streak
